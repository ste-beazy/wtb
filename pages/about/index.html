Hosted and created by TJ Cartwright, What’s the Biz is an energetic web series that highlights the stories of black business owners through storytelling!

With the help of Arsun Productions, WTB premiered in the Fall of 2019 with the mission to spread the news about all black owned businesses in Dayton, OH. As of now WTB is entering their 3rd season, has interviewed over 40 businesses, and has expanded as a national platform!

WTB is more than a talk show, it’s a community. Each member of the Biz Family understands the significance of shopping with black owned businesses. Our goal is to create a unique and exciting experience for our audience and business owners.
